Main Features

Improved flexibility

Using the app helps hire professionals from far away, because the photos are uploaded to the cloud and, using a secure password, can be downloaded anywhere.

Lower costs

Using the app, you can hire people from cheaper regions in the country, and even in the world, without any hassle. Why hire in rich states when you can hire from a poor state or country, paying less and giving work opportunities for the people who need them the most.

Greater Accesibility

The high resolution photos never leave the client's company. The professional doing the job only has access to photos in a resolution high enough to recognize who's who and which photos are good or bad, but HD enough for professional standards printing.

Increased Productivity

The speed of a professional using the app is more than 5000 photos/hour, against an average os 2000 photos per day doing the job by hand.

Easy to use

No more worrying about dragging, Ctrl+X and Ctrl+V. The folder sorting is entirely handled by the app on the background. Using the app, you only have to worry about which photos are good and which ones are not.

Precision

The face recognition precision we use has an average correct recognition rate of 98% with less than 0.01% of false recognitions, greater than the average human ability.
